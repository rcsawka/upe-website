Invitations

UPE is an invitation-only society. An undergraduate or graduate student is eligible for invitation to join the Mass. Alpha Chapter if:

  • Their major is Computer Science
  • Their GPA within CS meets a distinguished minimum
  • Their overall GPA meets a distinguished minimum
  • They have made significant progress towards a CS degree

More details can be found in our bylaws (see Article III).
